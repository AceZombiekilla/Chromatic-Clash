Chromatic Clash Online v20 - Yellow Trap Return + Hover + Popup Controls

Updates:
- When Yellow leaves the field, any trapped cards return to their owner's field and retrigger their Field of Play ability.
- Hover over a Yellow card with trapped cards to see what is trapped underneath it.
- Hover over either discard pile to preview all cards in that discard pile.
- Added a Show Popup button and Hide Popup option so you can hide choice/reaction popups, look at the field, then bring the popup back.
- Keeps online host/join, reaction chains, bot play, sounds, and instant rematch.
